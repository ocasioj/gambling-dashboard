📋 How to Log Your Bets

Natural language (CLI)

python3 log_user_bet.py --parse "bet $25 Celtics -110 vs Lakers nba" python3 log_user_bet.py --parse "bet $20 over 224.5 nba"

Structured (CLI)

python3 log_user_bet.py --stake 25 --pick "Celtics" --odds -110 --sport nba python3 log_user_bet.py --update 3 --outcome win

Via Joe (natural language)

Tell Joe: "log a $25 bet on Celtics -110 spread"

Export

python3 log_user_bet.py --export my_bets.csv
